About Me

I am currently a research fellow (postdoctoral) at Nanyang Technological University, working under the supervision of Prof. Kwok Yan Lam. Previously, I was a research officer (postdoctoral) at the University of Sydney, where I worked under the guidance of Prof. Qiang Tang and Prof. Vincent Gramoli. In August 2023, I completed my PhD in Computer Science at the University of Sydney, supervised by Dr. Qiang Tang. Before that, I was enrolled in the PhD program at the New Jersey Institute of Technology from September 2017 to January 2021. I earned both a master's and a bachelor's degree in mathematics from Hefei University of Technology in 2014 and 2017, respectively.

Research Interests

My research interests primarily focus on Blockchain, Consensus, Distributed Computing/Cryptography, Byzantine Storage, and Order Fairness.
