About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the CS Department at CityUHK. Prior to this, I was a postdoc at NTU (Oct. 2024 – Aug. 2025), working with Prof. Kwok Yan Lam. Before that, I was a postdoc at USYD (Oct. 2023 – Sep. 2024), working with Prof. Qiang Tang and Prof. Vincent Gramoli. I completed my PhD at USYD in Aug. 2023, supervised by Prof. Qiang Tang, after being enrolled in the PhD program at NJIT from 2017 to 2021. I earned both my BSc and MSc in Mathematics from HFUT.

Research Interests

My research interests primarily focus on Blockchain, Consensus, Distributed Computing/Cryptography, Byzantine Storage, and Order Fairness.
