About Me

I am currently a research officer (postdoctoral) at the University of Sydney, under the supervision of Dr. Qiang Tang and Dr. Vincent Gramoli. In August 2022, I earned my PhD degree from the University of Sydney, supervised by Dr. Qiang Tang. Prior to that, I had been enrolled in the PhD program at the New Jersey Institute of Technology from September 2017 to January 2021. I hold both a master's and a bachelor's degree in Mathematics from Hefei University of Technology.

Research Interests

My research interests primarily focus on Blockchain, Consensus, Distributed Computing/Cryptography, Byzantine Storage, and Order Fairness.
